Education

B.S. Earth Science, Data Analytics Concentration (With Honors)

UC Santa Cruz  ·  Graduated June 2020
Thesis · Presented at AGU

Assessing Long-Term Changes in Sea Surface Temperature Extremes

I used Generalized Extreme Value (GEV) distribution modeling on 36 years of global daily sea surface temperature data (NOAA-OISST, 1982-2018) to find which regions are seeing the biggest changes in SST extremes. High-latitude regions came out on top, especially the seas around northern Russia and Northern Europe, which are seeing the fastest rise in both the mean and variability of temperature extremes. I also tested how the Arctic Oscillation and ENSO influence these extremes. Co-authored with my UCSC advisors Claudie Beaulieu and Katerina Giamalaki.
